About Us

GreenPledge is a virtual tree plantation initiative that aims to promote environmental awareness and encourage individuals to make a real impact by supporting tree planting efforts.

Through our platform, users can contribute donations which go directly to verified NGOs involved in real-world afforestation projects. For every donation made, we generate a personalized certificate to recognize and celebrate your contribution.

Our mission is to make tree planting accessible to everyone from the comfort of their home while ensuring transparency, trust, and real-world results.
