About Us

GreenPledge is a virtual tree plantation initiative that promotes environmental awareness and encourages individuals to support real-world tree planting efforts.

Your donations go directly to verified NGOs that plant trees across the country. Every donor receives a certificate for their valuable contribution.

We proudly collaborate with trusted environmental organizations including:

  • 🌿 EarthRoots Initiative
  • 🌱 GoGreen Society
  • 🍀 EcoFriends Alliance
  • 🌾 Reforest India Trust

Together, we are committed to making the world greener and healthier for future generations.
